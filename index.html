How It Works

User Action

A user (you) selects the type of sentiment they would like to “comment” on social media. They may write a comment themselves, or, if they are struggling, they may click a button for a random comment. It will populate a positive or negative comment, depending on the button selected. This user interaction is to simulate the commenting of real-time users on social media.

Code Reaction

Our program takes the user’s submission and analyzes the words you use to determine if the sentence holds a positive, negative, or neutral sentiment. The program then applies an emoji that aligns with the positive, negative, or neutral sentiment and uses it as a visual representation of the sentence’s sentiment type. This is the comment analysis that is applied to social media.

Sentiment Conclusion

This program is a demo of our software that analyzes comments on social media to determine if users are commenting positively or negatively on user’s posts. The goal is to lessen the social media and internet bullying that occurs in today’s day and age. Companies and platforms can use our program to target comments that need to go under review for cyberbullying or libel.
